4. Click **"Upgrade"**
5. Enter payment information

## Downgrading Your Plan

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to **Subscription** page
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select lower plan
4. Review limitations
5. Confirm downgrade
6. Takes effect next billing cycle

### What Happens

**Exceeding New Limits:**

If you exceed new plan limits:
```
Example: Downgrading PRO â†’ FREE

Current Usage:
- 75 menu items (limit: 50)
- 12 categories (limit: 10)
- 3 users (limit: 1)

Required Actions:
1. Delete or archive 25 menu items
2. Consolidate to 10 categories
3. Remove 2 team members

System will prompt you to comply before downgrade.
```

**Feature Loss:**

Features lost on downgrade:
- Analytics data (preserved but not accessible)
- Audit trail (preserved but not accessible)
- Export capability
- API access
- Additional locations
- Team members

**Data Preservation:**

Your data is preserved:
- Menu items (up to new limit)
- Categories (up to new limit)
- Historical data
- Images
- Settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.

### What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data is preserved but may not be accessible if it exceeds new plan limits. You'll need to reduce usage to comply with new limits.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. No cancellation fees. Access continues until end of billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer refunds within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied. Contact support for refund requests.

### Can I get a custom plan?
Yes, BUSINESS plan customers can request custom plans with specific needs. Contact sales for custom pricing.
